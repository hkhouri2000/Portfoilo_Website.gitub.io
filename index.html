About Me

As a prospective student for the BS in Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics program, I am highly interested in three specific areas: Artificial Intelligence (AI), quantum computing, and game theories. My skills and academic background in computer science and mathematics have prepared me to excel in these areas and make meaningful contributions to the field.

Firstly, AI is a fascinating field that has captured my attention and imagination. The potential for AI to revolutionize various industries, such as healthcare, transportation, education, and entertainment, makes it an exciting and dynamic field to be a part of. My academic background in computer science and mathematics has provided me with a strong foundation in key concepts such as machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision. Through hands-on experience, such as implementing a spam filter using naïve Bayes classification and investigating the use of data science and AI in wildlife tracking to combat elephant poaching, I have gained a deeper understanding of these concepts. My passion for AI, coupled with my academic background, makes me eager to contribute to the field and be a part of the larger picture.

Secondly, quantum computing is an area of interest to me due to its potential to solve problems that are beyond the capabilities of classical computers. My coursework in quantum computation has given me a strong understanding of the basics of quantum mechanics and how they can be applied to computation. I have also had the opportunity to work with quantum simulation software, which allows me to experiment with simple quantum algorithms and see how they behave on a quantum computer. This has given me a deeper understanding of the potential and limitations of quantum computation and has sparked my curiosity to learn more about this exciting field.

Finally, the game of theories is another area of interest to me within the mathematics major. The game of theories is a framework for understanding and comparing the relative strengths and limitations of different mathematical models or theories. It has applications in fields such as economics, physics, and computer science and can be used to study a wide range of phenomena, from the evolution of species to the behavior of markets. Through my coursework in the game of theories, I have gained a strong understanding of key concepts such as the minimax theorem, principle of induction, and the Nash equilibrium. These concepts have helped me to better understand how different theories can be applied to real-world situations and how they can be compared and evaluated based on their predictive power and other factors. I am particularly interested in using the game of theories to study the behavior of complex systems, such as social networks or economic markets, and to understand how different factors can influence the outcomes of these systems.

In summary, my passion for AI, quantum computing, and game theories, coupled with my academic background in computer science and mathematics, make me an excellent fit for the BS in Artificial Intelligence and Mathematics program. I am excited to continue exploring and learning in these fields and believe that my skills and interests make me a strong candidate for this program.
